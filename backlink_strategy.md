## 1. Objective

The primary objective of this backlink strategy is to **increase the domain authority (DA) of `fluentry.online`** by acquiring high-quality, relevant backlinks from Moroccan education sites, job portals, and university forums. A higher DA will significantly improve search engine rankings for high-intent keywords like "IELTS preparation Morocco" and city-specific terms, driving more organic traffic and leads.

## 2. Target Audience & Link Types

We will focus on acquiring two main types of backlinks:

| Link Type | Description | SEO Value |
|---|---|---|
| **Guest Posts** | Writing original, high-value articles for Moroccan education blogs or news sites. The article will contain a contextual link back to a relevant page on `fluentry.online`. | **High**. Provides a strong authority signal and referral traffic. |
| **Directory & Forum Listings** | Adding `fluentry.online` to relevant business directories, university resource pages, or student forum discussions. | **Medium**. Builds foundational authority and local relevance. |

## 5. Content Creation for Guest Posts

- **Focus on Value:** Each guest post must be genuinely useful and solve a problem for the target audience. It should not be a sales pitch.
- **Keyword Research:** Lightly season the guest post with relevant keywords, but prioritise natural language.
- **Contextual Link:** The link back to `fluentry.online` should be natural and relevant within the body of the text. For example, in an article about IELTS, a link to the `/ielts-preparation-morocco` page is perfect.
