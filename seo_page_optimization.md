## On-Page Optimization Checklist

### All Pages Must Have:
- ✅ Unique, keyword-rich title (50-60 characters)
- ✅ Compelling meta description (150-160 characters)
- ✅ H1 with primary keyword (only one per page)
- ✅ H2/H3 hierarchy with secondary keywords
- ✅ 2-3% keyword density (natural, not forced)
- ✅ Internal links to related pages (3-5 per page)
- ✅ Image alt text with keywords
- ✅ Canonical tag (self-referential)
- ✅ Structured data (FAQ, Course, Person, BreadcrumbList)
- ✅ Mobile-friendly layout
- ✅ Fast loading (Core Web Vitals optimized)

### Home Page Additional:
- ✅ FAQ schema (6+ questions)
- ✅ Organization schema
- ✅ LocalBusiness schema
- ✅ BreadcrumbList schema

### Blog Pages Additional:
- ✅ Article schema
- ✅ Author information
- ✅ Published/Modified dates
- ✅ Related posts links

### Pricing Page Additional:
- ✅ Course schema
- ✅ Offer schema
- ✅ FAQ schema
- ✅ Price comparison table
